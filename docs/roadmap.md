# Roadmap

This roadmap clearly separates what is built from what is planned. Do not remove wishlist items during active development.

## Current Maintenance
- Ongoing curation of the 34 remaining mappings in the Review Pack via the Admin Review tools.
- Manual verification of mathematical notations and diagram cropping for Physics Model 10 Q1-Q45.

## Planned / Wishlist
- **V1 Lite Student Workflows:** Activate the remaining 4 placeholder blocks on the `/v1` landing page (`q_only`, `q_with_key`, `q_with_llm`, scorecards). (Admin card & Triage view are active, password gate disabled).
- **Advanced Paper Ingestion:** Fully automated OCR/parsing of PDF exams to extract questions and diagrams without manual cleanup.
- **Dynamic Test Generation:** Allow students to auto-generate 50-question mock tests weighted by chapter importance or past mistakes.
- **Detailed Solutions:** Attach step-by-step logic and formula references to each question.
- **Subject Ingestion Expansion:** Full ingestion pipeline for Chemistry and Biology course material (currently placeholders in the Admin Course Material DB View).
- **Scorecards:** A visual dashboard for students showing their weak chapters and historical progression over time.
